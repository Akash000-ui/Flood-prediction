🧭 How This App Works

This system uses Google Earth Engine satellite data combined with a trained Random Forest machine learning model to predict flood risk for any location in real time. Simply click on the map or enter coordinates to get an instant flood probability score along with the underlying environmental factors.

📊 Parameter Flood Risk Thresholds

Understanding what each value means — when is a location at risk?

🌧️
7-Day Rainfall
Cumulative precipitation (mm)
✅ Safe < 50 mm
⚠️ Moderate 50 – 100 mm
🔴 High Risk > 100 mm
💧
Soil Moisture
Volumetric water content (m³/m³)
✅ Safe < 0.30 m³/m³
⚠️ Moderate 0.30 – 0.45 m³/m³
🔴 High Risk > 0.45 m³/m³
⛰️
Elevation
Height above sea level (m)
🔴 High Risk < 20 m
⚠️ Moderate 20 – 50 m
✅ Safe > 50 m

Low-lying areas accumulate water during heavy rainfall events

📐
Terrain Slope
Ground inclination (degrees)
🔴 High Risk < 2°
⚠️ Moderate 2° – 5°
✅ Safe > 5°

Flat terrain cannot drain water, increasing inundation risk

Overall Risk Score: 🟢 LOW < 40% 🟡 MEDIUM 40% – 70% 🔴 HIGH > 70% 📧 Email alert sent when risk ≥ 50%
